## The Winkler Model

The Winkler foundation model (also known as the elastic foundation model) is used in structural engineering to analyze beams resting on elastic foundations. This ML approach provides:

- Faster predictions than traditional numerical methods
- Good accuracy for design-stage estimates
- Potential for real-time applications in structural analysis tools
